ABOUT THE KING

TheIronKingVR is a Gorilla Tag creator whose life story is about courage, community, and finding his own path. He came out as gay to his family, left a tough fast-food job, and turned his love for VR into a platform that celebrates who he is. Along the way he found love with his girlfriend, who is transgender, and their relationship is a proud part of the journey.

Movement tech, update reactions, funny moments, and pure chaos in VR are the heart of the channel, but the journey from coming out to choosing creation — and sharing it with his trans girlfriend — is what really defines The Iron King.
